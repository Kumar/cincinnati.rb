Cincinnati Ruby Brigade

Objective

The time has come for a Cincinnati.rb.

Ruby rocks. Rails rocks. Hacking with a group of people rocks, it accelerates the learning curve. We created Cincinnati.rb to provide a frequent Ruby hacking opportunity to all those interested.

Come one, come all, it’s open hacking night every night — we’re going to let the meeting drive itself to wherever it wants to go.

Location

Our meeting location has changed!

We will be meeting in the SAP Lab conference room in the basement of the UC College of Business.

As you enter the building locate the elevators or stairs and go down to the basement. You should quickly stumble upon the SAP Lab with lots of little desks/tables and rooms surrounding the perimeter. In the back right corner of the lab is the conference room, we will be meeting there.

Parking

I imagine on-street parking in the area will be limited due to night classes, but you may be able to find a spot for free if you don’t mind a little walk. Otherwise there are two garages right near the College of Business, which you can pay to park. I’d recommend the Campus Green garage on your left if you turn in on Woodside Drive. There is also another garage on the right next to Langsam Library, but I cannot remember what it is called.

Do not park in Burnett Woods, the park on the other side of Martin Luther King Dr. Most of the parking there is for permit holders only and you will be ticketed, trust me.
